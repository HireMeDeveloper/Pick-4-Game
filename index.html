How To Play
Identify groups of four items that share
a common trait, and tap 'Submit' to check if you're correct.
Complete the groups without making
4 mistakes!

just '5-LETTER-WORDS', 'NAMES', or 'VERBS'.

multiple categories!

what we perceive to be difficult. These will be
revealed as you solve. Those assigned to the
